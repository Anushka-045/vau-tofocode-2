Chocolate
Lava Cake

25 min prep
4 servings
450 kcal
Cooking progress 0 / 6 steps
25:00 remaining

🧂 Ingredients

  • 115g dark chocolate (70%+)
  • 115g unsalted butter
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 60g caster sugar
  • 30g plain flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch flaky sea salt

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. 01

    Prep your ramekins

    Preheat the oven to 220°C (200°C fan). Generously butter 4 individual ramekins (150ml) and dust with cocoa powder, tapping out the excess.

  2. 02

    Melt chocolate & butter

    Break the chocolate into pieces and place in a heatproof bowl with the butter. Melt over a pan of barely simmering water (bain-marie), stirring occasionally until smooth. Remove from heat and set aside to cool slightly.

  3. 03

    Whisk eggs & sugar

    In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, egg yolks, and caster sugar together until the mixture is pale, thick, and has doubled in volume — about 3 minutes with an electric whisk.

  4. 04

    Combine & fold

    Pour the cooled chocolate mixture into the egg mixture and gently fold together using a spatula. Sift in the flour and fold until just combined — do not overmix.

  5. 05

    Fill & chill

    Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins. At this point you can refrigerate them for up to 24 hours — just add 2 minutes to the bake time if cooking from cold.

  6. 06

    Bake & serve immediately

    Place ramekins on a baking tray and bake for 10–12 minutes until the tops are set but the centres still wobble. Run a knife around the edge, invert onto a warm plate, and serve at once with vanilla ice cream.
